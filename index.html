About

EKOWISATA ARUMSARI

Arumsari Ecotourism in Wonosari Village, Malang, East Java, offers a unique camping experience amidst breathtaking natural beauty. With a strong focus on ecotourism and sustainability, Arumsari provides camping facilities and stunning photo spots, all while promoting environmental conservation. Visitors can enjoy activities like hiking, stargazing, and exploring nature, enhancing their appreciation for the environment.
